# Phishing Prevention Checklist

## How to Prevent Phishing Attacks

- Do not click on suspicious links in emails or messages  
- Verify the sender's email address before responding  
- Avoid sharing personal information online  
- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)  
- Keep software and antivirus updated  
- Check website URLs carefully before entering details  
- Avoid downloading attachments from unknown sources  
- Report suspicious emails to authorities  
- Stay informed about common cyber threats  

## Importance of Prevention

Following these preventive measures helps protect personal data and reduces the risk of cybercrime. Awareness is the first step toward digital safety.
