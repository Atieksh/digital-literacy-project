# Social Media Etiquette Checklist

## Responsible Social Media Usage

- Think before posting content online
- Avoid sharing personal or sensitive information
- Respect others while commenting or posting
- Use strong passwords for social media accounts
- Enable privacy settings for better security
- Avoid posting misleading or false information
- Do not share offensive or inappropriate content
- Maintain a professional online presence
- Avoid cyberbullying or harassment
- Report suspicious or harmful content

## Importance of Social Media Etiquette

Following proper social media etiquette helps maintain a safe and respectful online environment. It protects personal information and promotes responsible digital behavior.
